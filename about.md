## Short bio
I am a demographer and postdoctoral researcher at the **Helsinki Institute for Demography and Population Health** (University of Helsinki), affiliated with the **Max Planck–University of Helsinki Centre for Social Inequalities in Population Health**.

My research focuses on inequalities in mental health, mortality, and living conditions, often using population registers and longitudinal designs that allow careful interpretation and policy-relevant inference.

## Education
- **PhD in Demography (2019–2023)**, Centre for Demographic Research, UCLouvain (Belgium).  
  Dissertation: *“Unlivable. Housing conditions, residential mobility and suicide of the working-age population. An analysis of Belgian administrative data.”*
- **MSc in Population and Development Studies (2016–2018)**, UCLouvain (Highest distinction).  
  Thesis: *“Housing conditions and mortality in Belgium, 1991–2016.”*
- **BA in Political Sciences (2014–2016)**, UCLouvain–FUCAM (Distinction).
